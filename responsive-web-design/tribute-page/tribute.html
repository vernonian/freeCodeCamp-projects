Circa Survive

Circa Survive is an American rock band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, that formed in 2004. The band, led and founded by Anthony Green, consists of former members from This Day Forward and Taken.

Timeline

  • Formation and The Inuit Sessions (2004–2005)
  • Juturna (2005–2006)
  • On Letting Go (2007–2008)
  • Blue Sky Noise and Appendage (2008–2010)
  • Violent Waves (2012–2014)
  • Descensus (2014–2016)
  • The Amulet (2017–2021)
  • Two Dreams and hiatus (2021–2022)
